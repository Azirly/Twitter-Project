1. Enter in your username and password. If it is wrong, the program will end.

2. Start Menu Commands:
* "entire timeline"
    - Prints out every tweets on Twitter starting from tweetId #1.
* "show all users"
    - Prints out every user and his/her corresponding id on Twitter.
* "show all my followers"
    - Prints out a list of the current user's followers and their corresponding ids
* "home timeline"
    - Prints out the last time tweets on Twitter.
* "searching"
    - Prints out all tweets that have the search word specified by the user
* "tweeting"
    - Creates a new tweet for the current user. Requires a body of text and list of hashtags
